About B. P Trading

For over 50 years, B. P Trading Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of textile manufacturing and wholesale fabric supply in Kolkata, India. We specialize in producing high-quality fabrics including Cotton, Rayon, Lycra, Polyester, Cotswool, and Lafar in various styles.

Our fabrics are expertly crafted for diverse applications including men's kurtas, women's kurtis, dresses, blouses, nightwear, and kidswear. We collaborate with India's finest weavers, dyeing mills, and printing units to deliver customized, premium fabrics to retailers, wholesalers, and leading brands.

With decades of experience and unwavering commitment to quality, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner in the textile industry.
